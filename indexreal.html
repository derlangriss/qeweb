About Queen Sirikit Entomoloy Section

QSBG Entomology Section (QE) is the one section of research and development in Queen Sirikit Botanic Garden

In more recent times QSBG has become more active in entomological research in Thailand aimed at developing taxonomic and biodiversity inventory skills in Thailand.
